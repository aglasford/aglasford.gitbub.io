Amiko Glasford

Bio

Amiko worked in finance for nine years before switching careers to focus on startups and technology companies. She is interested in startups and technology that change human behavior and/or improve the human condition. At her core, she believes in people and our power to change the world around us for the better.

Amiko takes great pleasure in eating dessert, power lifting, dancing and completing obstacle course races. She also enjoys serendipitous encounters, international travel, sassy old women, and volunteering with children. She has her BA and MBA from Harvard University and lives in New York.
